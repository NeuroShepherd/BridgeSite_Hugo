Point of Care

BRIDGE is integrated with the UCSF medical record, which is an Epic system. Clinicians can click on a link directly from the patient's medical record to launch the BRIDGE dashboard.

Interactive

BRIDGE currently has multiple ways to visualize data, called widgets, that we continue to expand by engaging with clinicians, patients and scientists.

Real-time

BRIDGE pulls from multiple data sources live - from the medical record, as well as varied research, patient-facing, wearable, and other platforms.

Actionable

BRIDGE displays actionable visualizations to support clinical decision making.

Configurable

BRIDGE is a configurable platform built to easily create clinic specific dashboards. Clinicians can mix and match between existing data sources and widgets.

Innovative

BRIDGE is built to bring research, algorithms and innovation to the point of care.
